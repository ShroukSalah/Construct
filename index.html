BIM Services

We Generate 3D Models from the Contract Drawings. Coordinated models using BIM allow our clients to analyze possible interference among all building systems. Clash Detection can be done virtually thus saving money in Field on labor & material.

- Regarding clash coordination, Computer models are used to validate designs and coordinate systems. Clash Detection Tool is used to notify all the parties/trades about the potential conflicts. These Clashes/Conflicts can be resolved virtually in the Model vs out in the Field.

- For bill of quantities, BIM Process provides an excellent opportunity to calculate the BOQ. The Quantity take-off can be generated from the well-coordinated models.

- For shop drawings, the conceptual designs and specifications are coordinated into BIM model & Shop Drawings are produced out of this Model. These Shop Drawings are the outcome of the well-coordinated BIM Process; these can be used to start the layouts in the field & Construction can be done using the information in the shop drawings.

- For BIM coordination, we Provide our Services to be a part of the BIM coordination Meetings on behalf of our client. BIM meeting lead services are also provided upon the request of the Contractor.
